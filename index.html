ABOUT US

COUNSEL LEGAL PRACTICE is a fully serviced law firm with broad expertise and experience in providing first-rate services across an array of legal practice areas to different clients that include domestic and international clients, government agencies and individuals. Our mission is to excel in client service, contribute to the development of the legal profession and society in general. We tackle challenging projects creatively, conduct legal research and due diligence painstakingly, and offer fearless representation to our clients. We are able to offer commercially astute and innovative advice, due to the high calibre of our lawyers and the depth of our knowledge and expertise across a wide range of industries.

Located in the heart of the Federal Capital Territory, our corporate office at House 3, 16A POW Mafemi Crescent, Utako, Abuja FCT, Nigeria is strategically situated thereby providing accessibility to existing and potential clients within the Federal Capital territory and its environs. In a consistently evolving business world, we constantly seek to satisfy our clients in ways that are not just cost-effective but result oriented. Hence we are glad to partner with you in ways that will advance your business objectives.
